About Me

Hello, I am an aspiring software developer who is currently enrolled in a software development bootcamp. I believe that life is a journey, and my journey, like those of countless others, has been full of all kinds of interesting experiences. Because everyone possesses such an eclectic collection of personal experiences, I believe that we are all capable of contributing something positive to the growth and well-being of others. No matter how insignificant you may feel, there is someone who needs what you have to offer. No matter how hopeless you may feel, you can always find the strength to take one more step. And no matter how broken you may think you are, there will be someone who has been praying for someone exactly like you to come into their life. Keep moving forward!

Things I Love

This is a Hawaiian BBQ plate. This is definitely one of the most delicious things I have ever eaten. It comes with some kind of meat, usually chicken katsu, BBQ chicken, beef, and if you're feeling fancy, fried shrimp or fish. It comes with a side of mac and veggies. I highly recommend you sub the rice for more mac. If they make you pay extra, they are probably terrible people. But get it anyways. And also doggy.

This is a burger. It has seasoned ground beef patties in between two buns with cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and special sauce. You usually order them with fries and a soda. I recommend you get diet soda because they taste the same minus like, 47g of sugar. Burgers are one of the most delicious things on the planet and life would be bleak and meaningless without them. And also doggy.

This is a pizza. It is dough topped with marinara sauce, cheese, and a variety of other toppings ranging from meats to vegetables. Even pineapple, which people think is the worst thing ever but I like it. This particular pizza is topped with pepperoni, sausage, and ham. Pizzas are truly one of the most delicious foods on the planet, and life would be a mundane, joyless facade without them. And also doggy.

This is a California Burrito. It comes [I think] from California. And is a burrito. It is a flour tortilla filled with carne asada, cheese, sour cream, and fries. This might be the most delicious thing I have ever eaten in my life. These changed my life, and I would not be who I am without them. Without these, life would be nothing more than a soulless, nihilistic march to our inevitable graves. And also doggy.
